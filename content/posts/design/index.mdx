custom colour theme.

  bar: string

  bar: 'baz',

### `<Concept />`

Sometimes, I want to highlight a concept in the body of a text, to
highlight a propper term, much like you would see illustrated with
small caps in a philosophy essay. I use my custom MDX `<Concept />`
component to achieve this. e.g. <Concept>Concept</Concept>.
